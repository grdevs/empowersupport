CIW Registered Mental Health Domicilliary Support Service

Empower Support is a nurse owned and led mental health domiciliary support service operating in and around the Swansea area. We saw the need for high quality, post hospital support for the most vulnerable and often forgotten members of society. 

We look beyond labels and believe that with the right, recovery focused, person centred support those living with a mental illness can have the opportunity to not only survive, but thrive in the community.

We accept referrals from the local authoritary, care co-ordinators, community mental health teams as well as direct payments from self referring service users. 

Our modern approach not only empowers the people we support to live their best, most fulfilling lives, but also acknowledges the key work our highly skilled staff carry out.

We believe

in the importance of individual, needs-based mental health care and rehabilitation with a community focus. We believe that humans were not designed to live in hospitals and institutions, and so we work to create a progressive and empowering springboard from which our service users can rebuild their lives.  


We look 

beyond symptoms and prescriptions to consider all aspects of quality of life and human experience, to provide our service users with the opportunity to not only recover but thrive. We look at human life as a series of opportunities and we endeavour for our service users to leave us and not just re-join the community, but become valid and enriched members of the community. 

We offer

an environment which encourages self-acceptance, meaningful occupation and purposeful recovery. We offer a professional understanding beyond the inherited medical model. We offer a psychologically informed environment to ensure service users are set up to succeed in their futures.

We are Empower

There is an inherent and deep-rooted need in our society to offer infinitely better care and support to our most vulnerable members. Mental health conditions can take everything from someone: their family, their job, their friends, their children. If a society has just one person in this position, then it’s a society that has failed. We are only as strong as our weakest link, and in a society that prides itself on power, expansion and profitability I truly believe we need to be reminded of that. We need to be reminded of our humanity.
